# The Birth of Evil

## Core Question

Where do secret societies come from, what do they believe, and how does the concept of "evil" itself originate in Western religious history?

## Lecture Type

Multi-topic deep dive — combines a sweeping history of Western religion (three stages), the orthodox vs. esoteric reading of the Bible, and a literary analysis of Milton's Paradise Lost as a secret society text.

## Central Thesis

The concept of "evil" as we understand it today was born with monotheism — specifically Christianity. Before monotheism, there was no concept of evil; there was only fortune, fate, and the immutable laws of the universe. The three revolutionary concepts that Christianity introduced — truth, evil, and the individual — are counterintuitive (unlike the intuitive mother goddess and polytheistic worldviews) and therefore had to be imposed by imperial force (Rome), which required destroying the older worldviews. Secret societies emerged as underground resistance movements to preserve the pre-Christian understanding that "mind leads to matter" — that consciousness, not material reality, is primary. The esoteric tradition reads the God of the Bible as a false god (the Demiurge), Satan as a truth-teller, and Jesus as a messenger from the true god (the Monad) sent to remind humanity of the divine spark within them.

## Argument Structure

### Theory 1: Orthodox Christian narrative
- **The theory:** One true God created the world; humans disobeyed; God progressively revealed His will through covenants; Jesus redeemed humanity from original sin
- **Evidence for:** The canonical Bible; church tradition; dominant Western civilisation narrative
- **Evidence against:** Internal contradictions (why banish for eating fruit? why destroy world if humans still wicked? why must God's son die?)
- **Verdict:** Prof. Jiang presents this as the "official version" but identifies logical problems; notes David likely wrote the Bible to legitimise his dynasty

### Theory 2: Esoteric/Gnostic narrative (what secret societies believe)
- **The theory:** The true God (Monad) is a cosmic mind whose emanations created the universe; a divine being (Sophia) made a mistake creating the Demiurge, who is the false god of the Bible; Jesus was sent by the Monad to reveal this truth; evil was born when monotheism suppressed older intuitive knowledge
- **Evidence for:** Internal consistency of the Bible itself (Genesis confirms Satan told the truth about the fruit); the logical problems with orthodox narrative disappear; aligns with "mind leads to matter" which was the dominant human worldview for most of history
- **Evidence against:** Not presented — Prof. Jiang presents this sympathetically as the secret society position
- **Verdict:** Prof. Jiang treats this as the more internally consistent reading and demonstrates it through direct Bible analysis

### Theory 3: Three stages of religious evolution
- **The theory:** Western religion evolved through Mother Goddess → Polytheism → Monotheism, each driven by material conditions (agriculture → war → empire)
- **Evidence for:** Archaeological record (fertility cults, goddess figurines); Greek pantheon hierarchy reflecting military outcomes; Roman imperial Christianity
- **Evidence against:** Not presented in this lecture
- **Verdict:** Prof. Jiang presents as historical framework; aligns with Civilization series lectures on mother goddess and religion

## Key Concepts & Frameworks

### The Three Stages of Western Religion
- **Mother Goddess civilisation:** Womb-centred, agricultural, communal, egalitarian, metaphorical worldview, fertility cults, bird + bull symbolism, no property, no hierarchy, no marriage
- **Polytheism:** War-driven, property-based, hierarchical, marriage institution, pantheon reflecting military outcomes, gods as playthings of fortune/fate, immutable laws of universe (justice)
- **Monotheism:** Empire-driven (Rome), truth/evil/individual as new concepts, counterintuitive (must be imposed by force), destroys older worldviews

### Mind Leads to Matter vs. Matter Leads to Mind
- Pre-Christian: mind creates the brain to understand the world (dominant view for most of human history)
- Modern: brain creates mind through neural connections (scientifically dominant but Prof. Jiang notes even top neuroscientists can't explain consciousness)

### Metaphorical vs. Literal World
- Ancient: emotions understood as gods seizing you; world interpreted through metaphor; produced extraordinary creativity (e.g. pyramids)
- Modern: literal interpretation; emotions as internal psychological states; less creative capacity

### The Three Concepts of Monotheism
- **Truth:** One true God → one truth; moving toward truth = good, away = evil
- **Evil:** New concept that didn't exist before; defying God = evil
- **The Individual:** Faith in one God supersedes community/family loyalty; can step outside community to do what's right
- These three concepts "underpin modernity" and "control our world"

### The Monad and the Demiurge (Gnostic cosmology)
- **Monad:** True god, divine sun, emanates vibrational energy that eventually becomes matter
- **Dyads:** First emanations, male-female pairs, can create new universes
- **Sophia:** A Dyad who tried to create alone, produced the Demiurge
- **Demiurge:** False god, monstrosity, believes he is the only god, creates Earth and humanity, is the God of the Old Testament
- **Divine spark:** Fragment of the Monad in every human; activated through goodness, love, rejection of materialism

### The Nephilim
- Children of angels ("sons of God") and human women
- Enslaved humanity; God flooded world to destroy them
- Divine beings cannot truly die → became demons in shadows
- Secret societies believe Nephilim still control the world today
- Biblical "heroes of old, warriors of renown" = Hercules, Achilles, etc.
- "Sons of God" = the old polytheistic gods reframed under monotheism

### Secret Society Lineage
- Fertility cults (Mother Goddess era, run by women) → Mystery Schools (women go underground when war/men take over) → Secret Societies (mystery schools go underground when Christianity/Empire threatens them)
- Core secret: mind leads to matter; consciousness creates reality
- Named: Knights Templar, Rosicrucians, Illuminati, Freemasons

### The Six Biblical Covenants
1. Adam: Paradise, don't eat the fruit
2. Noah: I'll never destroy the world again
3. Abraham: You and your descendants are chosen; the Promised Land
4. Moses: The Ten Commandments / Mosaic Law
5. David: Your house will rule Israel forever
6. Jesus: Original sin redeemed; everyone can now love God (starts Christianity)

## Historical Narratives & Examples

### The Origin of War (from Q&A)
- In mother goddess societies, women had high status
- As populations grew, surplus young men were discarded (women always had value)
- These young men formed "wolf packs" that raided other villages to capture women
- This is how war began — not from ideology but from surplus males seeking mates

### David as Bible Author
- Prof. Jiang suggests David (or his court) likely wrote the Bible
- The Davidic Covenant conveniently declares David's family will rule forever
- All five covenants build toward David being "the person God has been looking for for eternity"

### Milton and Paradise Lost
- John Milton: rebel, free thinker, advocate of free speech, member of secret societies
- Wrote Paradise Lost while blind (dictated to secretaries — "if a blind man can recite poetry, he must be a prophet")
- National epic of the British Empire; elite members memorised it
- Foundational text of many secret societies
- Satan's first speech: heroic volunteer to escape Hell alone (used as initiation ceremony)
- Satan's second speech: temptation of Eve — orthodox reading says Satan lies; esoteric reading says Satan tells the truth and the Bible confirms it

### Genesis Reading — Satan Tells the Truth
- Serpent says: "You will not die... your eyes will be opened"
- After eating: "Then the eyes of both were opened" (confirmed)
- God says: "See, the man has become like one of us" (confirmed — Satan was truthful)
- God's real fear: humans might also eat from Tree of Life and become immortal
- Two trees: Knowledge (mind of God) + Life (immortality) — together they would make humans equal to God

### The Avengers Thought Experiment (for Nephilim)
- Imagine superheroes defeat a villain and save the world
- Then they get bored, have lots of children
- Children become demigods who enslave humanity
- Then fight among themselves
- This is essentially the Nephilim story
