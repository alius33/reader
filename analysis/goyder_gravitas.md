# Gravitas — Caroline Goyder
## Central Thesis
Gravitas — the quality of being taken seriously, of commanding attention and respect — is not a personality trait but a set of learnable skills rooted in breath, voice, body, and preparation. Goyder, a former voice coach at London's Central School of Speech and Drama, argues that most people lose their natural gravitas through anxiety, self-consciousness, and shallow breathing. The path back is through reconnecting with your physical presence: deeper breathing, grounded posture, slower speech, and authentic connection with your material and audience.
