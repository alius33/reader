# Emotional Intelligence — Daniel Goleman
## Central Thesis
IQ accounts for only about 20% of the factors that determine life success. The rest is largely explained by "emotional intelligence" — the ability to recognise, understand, manage, and effectively use emotions in yourself and others. Goleman's five domains: Self-Awareness, Self-Regulation, Motivation, Empathy, and Social Skills. EQ, unlike IQ, can be developed throughout life.
## Key Frameworks: Five Domains of EQ, Amygdala Hijack, Emotional Self-Awareness → Self-Regulation → Motivation → Empathy → Social Skills (a progression), The Marshmallow Test (delayed gratification predicts life outcomes)
