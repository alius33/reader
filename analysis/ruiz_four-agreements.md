# The Four Agreements — Don Miguel Ruiz
## Central Thesis
Most of our suffering comes from agreements we made unconsciously as children — beliefs about ourselves and the world that we absorbed from family, culture, and society. These agreements govern our behaviour and create a "dream" that is mostly a nightmare. Freedom comes from breaking these old agreements and replacing them with four new ones that eliminate most unnecessary suffering.
## The Four Agreements: 1) Be impeccable with your word, 2) Don't take anything personally, 3) Don't make assumptions, 4) Always do your best
