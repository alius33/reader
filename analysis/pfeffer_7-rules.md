# 7 Rules of Power — Jeffrey Pfeffer
## Central Thesis
Power is learnable. Most people fail to acquire it not because they lack talent but because they follow "leadership industry" myths about authenticity and servant leadership. The seven rules: Get out of your own way, Break the rules, Appear powerful, Build a powerful brand, Network relentlessly, Use your power, and Success excuses (almost) everything. Pfeffer's most blunt and practical book.
## Key Frameworks: Seven Rules, The Leadership Industry Critique (leadership books are aspirational fiction, not empirical description), Performance-Power Disconnect (revisited from Power)
