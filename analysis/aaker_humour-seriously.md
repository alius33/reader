# Analysis: Humour, Seriously — Jennifer Aaker & Naomi Bagdonas

## Book Metadata
- **Title:** Humour, Seriously
- **Subtitle:** Why Humour Is a Superpower at Work and in Life
- **Authors:** Jennifer Aaker & Naomi Bagdonas
- **Year:** 2020
- **Publisher:** Penguin Business / Currency (Random House)
- **Type:** Non-taxonomy, argument + how-to hybrid
- **Foreword by:** Ed Catmull (former president of Pixar)

## Key Frameworks

1. **The Humour Cliff** — Gallup data showing laughter drops off at age 23
2. **Four Deadly Humour Myths** — Serious Business, Failure, Being Funny, Born With It
3. **Four Humour Styles** — Stand-up, Sweetheart, Magnet, Sniper (2 axes: affiliative/aggressive x expressive/subtle)
4. **Levity-Humour-Comedy Spectrum** — Movement analogy (levity=movement, humour=exercise, comedy=sport)
5. **Humour Hormone Cocktail** — Dopamine, oxytocin, cortisol reduction, endorphins
6. **Four Benefits Framework** — Power, Bonds, Creativity, Resilience
7. **Truth + Misdirection** — Foundation of all humour (Incongruity-Resolution Theory)
8. **Five Mining Techniques** — Incongruity, Emotion, Opinion, Pain, Delight
9. **Five Forming Techniques** — Exaggerate, Create Contrast, Use Specifics, Make Analogies, Rule of Three
10. **Appropriate vs Funny Matrix** — 4-quadrant chart (appropriate/inappropriate x funny/not-funny)
11. **Truth-Pain-Distance** — Anne Libera's comedy theory for navigating gray areas
12. **Humour Fail Lifecycle** — Recognize → Diagnose → Make It Right
13. **Three Culture Archetypes** — Instigators, Culture Carriers, Hidden Gems
14. **Peak-End Rule** — Defining moments in culture (curate peaks, design endings)
15. **Five Wishes of the Dying** — Boldness, Authenticity, Presence, Joy, Love
