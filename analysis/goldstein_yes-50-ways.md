# Yes! 50 Scientifically Proven Ways to Be Persuasive — Goldstein, Martin & Cialdini
## Central Thesis
Fifty short chapters, each describing one research-backed persuasion technique. Co-authored by Cialdini, it's essentially the practical companion to Influence — where Influence explains the six principles, Yes! gives you 50 specific applications across business, relationships, and daily life. Each chapter follows the format: research study → finding → practical application.
## Key Techniques (selection): Hotel towel reuse signs (social proof), emphasizing what people will LOSE (loss aversion), the power of because, reciprocity through personalisation, the labelling technique, the foot-in-the-door progression
## Cross-References: Influence - Cialdini (the theoretical foundation), Pre-Suasion - Cialdini (timing), Nudge concepts
