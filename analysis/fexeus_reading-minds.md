# The Art of Reading Minds — Henrik Fexeus
## Central Thesis
A Swedish mentalist's guide to reading people and influencing them through rapport-building, body language reading, sensory acuity, and psychological techniques. Fexeus argues that "mind reading" is not supernatural — it's the systematic observation of nonverbal cues, calibration to individual patterns, and the use of rapport to create unconscious connection. Combines NLP-influenced techniques with practical body language and cold reading methods.
## Key Frameworks: Rapport (matching and mirroring), Sensory Acuity (noticing micro-expressions and subtle cues), Representational Systems (visual/auditory/kinesthetic), Anchoring, Cold Reading techniques
