# The Culture Code — Daniel Coyle
## Central Thesis
Great group culture is not about genius individuals — it is about the signals of connection, belonging, and shared vulnerability that allow a group to cooperate at extraordinary levels. Three skills drive successful group cultures: Build Safety (signals of connection that generate belonging), Share Vulnerability (habits of mutual risk that drive trusting cooperation), and Establish Purpose (narratives that create shared goals and values).
## Key Frameworks: Three Skills (Safety, Vulnerability, Purpose), Belonging Cues, Vulnerability Loops, High-Purpose Environments
## Best Stories: Google's Project Aristotle (psychological safety trumps talent), Navy SEALs, Pixar's BrainTrust, San Antonio Spurs, IDEO, Tony Hsieh's downtown Vegas project, the Nyquist of a group (informal connectors)
