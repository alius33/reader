# Geo-Strategy — Prof. Jiang Xueqin

> A 12-lecture series on contemporary geopolitics — focusing on the Middle East conflict, American empire, Russian strategy, and the emerging multipolar world. Prof. Jiang applies historical patterns from his Civilization series to analyse current events, with particular focus on asymmetrical warfare, imperial hubris, and the decline of American hegemony.

---

## About the Lecturer

Prof. Jiang Xueqin brings a historian's perspective to contemporary geopolitics. He analyses current events through the same frameworks he uses for ancient civilizations — empire cycles, hubris, asymmetric strategy, and the tension between military dominance and strategic flexibility. His Geo-Strategy lectures are explicitly connected to his Civilization series, applying its historical lessons to the present.
