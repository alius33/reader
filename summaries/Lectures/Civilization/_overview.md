# Civilization — Prof. Jiang Xueqin

> A 60-lecture journey through human civilization — from the agricultural revolution to the decline of the American empire. Prof. Jiang traces the forces that built and destroyed the world's great civilizations: religion, empire, hubris, and the recurring patterns of rise and fall.

---

## About the Lecturer

Prof. Jiang Xueqin teaches history with a focus on the grand narrative — why civilizations rise, peak, and collapse. His lectures draw on archaeology, anthropology, psychology, and primatology to build arguments, always grounding abstract ideas in specific historical evidence and vivid stories. He structures every lecture around a driving question and evaluates competing theories before arriving at his preferred explanation.
