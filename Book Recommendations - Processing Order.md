## Tier 1 — High Priority (Process First)

These are foundational books with the highest return on insight. Each one changes how you think or act in a broadly applicable way.

### 1A. Influence & Persuasion

| # | Title | Author | Why It Matters |
|---|-------|--------|----------------|
| 1 | **Influence** | Robert Cialdini | The foundational text on persuasion — 6 principles (reciprocity, commitment, social proof, authority, liking, scarcity) that govern human compliance |
| 2 | **Pre-Suasion** | Robert Cialdini | Cialdini's sequel — how to set up influence *before* you make the ask. The moment before the message matters more than the message |
| 3 | **What Every Body Is Saying** | Joe Navarro | Ex-FBI body language reading. Practical, observation-based skill you use every day |
| 4 | **How To Win Friends And Influence People** | Dale Carnegie | The original social skills manual. 90 years old and still the most broadly applicable book on human relations |

### 1B. Psychology & Decision Making

| # | Title | Author | Why It Matters |
|---|-------|--------|----------------|
| 5 | **Thinking in Bets** | Annie Duke | Decision-making under uncertainty — separating decision quality from outcome quality |
| 6 | **The Psychology of Money** | Morgan Housel | How emotions and biases shape financial decisions. Applicable far beyond money |
| 7 | **Antifragile** | Nassim Nicholas Taleb | Systems that gain from disorder. Changes how you think about risk, resilience, and optionality |
| 8 | **You Are Not So Smart** | David McRaney | Catalogue of cognitive biases with engaging stories — essential self-awareness tool |
| 9 | **Noise** | Cass R. Sunstein & Daniel Kahneman | Why human judgments vary so wildly and what to do about it |

### 1C. Self-Mastery & Mindset

| # | Title | Author | Why It Matters |

| 10 | **Deep Work** | Cal Newport | The skill of focused concentration in a distracted world. Complements *So Good They Can't Ignore You* |

| 12 | **Man's Search for Meaning** | Viktor Frankl | Finding purpose in suffering. Short, profound, universally applicable |
| 13 | **Meditations** | Marcus Aurelius | 2,000-year-old Stoic journal. The original self-help book |
| 14 | **The Four Agreements** | Don Miguel Ruiz | Four deceptively simple rules that eliminate most unnecessary suffering |

### 1D. Strategy & Leadership

| # | Title | Author | Why It Matters |
|---|-------|--------|----------------|
